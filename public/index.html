What We Do

We set up an Italian restaurant, with several types of pasta,
both for those who eat meat and those who prefer a more
vegetarian option. The restaurant brings not only a great
quality cuisine, but also the experience of the space, involving
the Italian culture, with music, service, trying to make
customers really feel in Italy.

About Us

We are two friends who met in Ireland, Cork specifically, on a cultural exchange. After having visited many Italian restaurants in several countries, as well as in Italy, we decided to build our own business and bring some of the experience we had and set up our own restaurant, and pass this wonderful experience to our customers.
